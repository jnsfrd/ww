Workshop

An online workshop for data scientists and ML engineers focusing on federated machine learning with a step-by-step demonstration.


Information

In this workshop, we will guide you through the workflow of establishing a cross-silo ML federation within an organization.

Additionally, we will provide an in-depth exploration of the capabilities of FEDn Studio and demonstrate how it seamlessly integrates with MLOps workflows.

Agenda (Approximately 90 minutes):
  • Introductions and framework overview (15 min)
  • Demonstration of a practical use case (50 min)
  • Questions and answers (15 min)

Please indicate in the registration form if you wish to participate as a federated client in the practical segment of the workshop.

Familiarity with basic terminal commands, as well as a local environment with Docker and Git, is required.
